Hi there! Thanks for stopping by my homepage!

I am a 1st-year Ph.D. student in Computer Science at CM Lab (AIMM Group), National Taiwan University, advised by Prof. Wen-Huang Cheng. Concurrently, I am a Research Assistant at CITI, Academia Sinica, working with Prof. Jun-Cheng Chen. I received my Bachelor's degree in Computer Science from the National Taiwan University of Science and Technology (Taiwan Tech) in 2021, followed by an M.S. degree in Computer Science from the same institution in 2023, under the supervision of Prof.Kai-Lung Hua.

My primary research interests broadly include computer vision, vision-language understanding, deepfake forensics, and agentic AI.
